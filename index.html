How To Play

Basics/Goals

The main objective of the game is to find locations on the map, and place a Marker near the levels location. Locations are found via the "Scan" ability. You will get 10 scans per level. When you think you have found the location, you can place your marker where you think the locaiton is, if you are within a small square around the location you will score a point, if not, you will not get a point. To win the game, you have to find at least 5 locations. You can also click on the locations marker for a little fact on why I chose that location.

Scan

The Scan ability is your only way to find locations without random guessing. When you scan, you be notified in the "Scan Results" box if the levels location is within the bounds of the screen. This ability can be used 10 times per location.

Confirm Marker

This will confirm you level marker in the place it is, if it is close enought to the designated location, you will score a point.

Cheat

This should NEVER BE USED!!!!!! But if you do you is, it will reveal the levels location and give you a point.
